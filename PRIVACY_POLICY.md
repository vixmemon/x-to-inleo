# Privacy Policy - X to InLeo Extension

**Last Updated:** January 2024

## Overview

X to InLeo is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect

1. **Hive Username** - Stored locally in your browser to maintain your login session
2. **X/Twitter Post Content** - Text, images, videos, and polls extracted from posts you choose to share
3. **Browser Storage** - Session data stored locally on your device

### What We Do NOT Collect

- Personal identification information (name, email, address, phone number)
- Financial or payment information
- Location data or IP addresses
- Web browsing history
- Keystroke logs or user activity tracking
- Any data beyond what is necessary for the extension's core functionality

## Data Usage

Your data is used solely for:
- Maintaining your login session with your Hive account
- Extracting and sharing X/Twitter post content to the InLeo blockchain
- Enabling you to post content you select

## Data Storage

- **Hive Username** - Stored locally in your browser's local storage
- **Post Content** - Processed locally and transmitted only to the Hive blockchain API for posting
- **No Remote Servers** - We do not store your data on external servers

## Third-Party Services

This extension interacts with:
- **Hive Keychain** - For secure authentication (you control your private keys)
- **Hive Blockchain API** - To post content to InLeo
- **X/Twitter** - To extract post content from pages you visit

## Your Rights

- You can delete your stored Hive username at any time by logging out
- All data is stored locally on your device
- You have full control over what content you share

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. Any changes will be reflected in the extension's updated version.

## Contact

For privacy concerns, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store extension page.
